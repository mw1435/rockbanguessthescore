Competition rules & terms
How it works
Enter your prediction for the final score of the match. Each guess costs €2 (or as part of a bundle). Payment must be made via Revolut using your unique reference code. Unpaid entries are not eligible for the draw or prize.

Prize
The prize fund is shared equally between all entrants who correctly predict the exact final score (goals and points for both teams). If no entry matches the exact score, two entries are selected at random from all paid entries to share the prize equally.

Draw
The draw is conducted electronically and is final. Results are announced on this page. Rockbán GAA's decision is final in all matters relating to this competition.

Eligibility
This competition is open to players, parents and friends of Rockbán GAA. Entries must be submitted and paid before the closing time shown above.

Data & privacy
Personal data collected (name, phone, optional email) is used solely to administer this competition and will be deleted following the draw. Rockbán GAA is the data controller under GDPR. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting secretary@rockbangaa.ie.

Responsible gambling
This is a fundraising competition run by a voluntary GAA club. Please only enter amounts you are comfortable with.
